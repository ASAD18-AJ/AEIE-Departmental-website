About Department

The Applied Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering curriculum includes courses on the design and development of various analog and digital electronic devices used for measurement, monitoring, recording and control of several parameters such as flow, pressure, temperature, moisture, level, etc. and the calibration of such instruments. The students admitted here learn to program microprocessors and microcontrollers, for different applications and implementation of communication networks composed of sensors, actuators, Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) and Distributed Control System (DCS). Since instrumentation engineering is closely related to control engineering, courses on signal processing, control theory and intelligent control such as fuzzy control and neural networks are also included. The curriculum also encompasses various specialized fields such as biomedical & analytical instrumentation, electrical & electronic measurement etc.
The scope of this discipline is vast, and is fast growing particularly due to the various emerging automation excellence of the present world. Growth is also related to the development of more accurate as well as robust sensors with much higher precision than what we could have even imagined a generation ago.The Department of AEIE has a strong role to play towards research and development. The faculties of this department are very active in research. Some of the research areas of the faculties include Internet of things (IoT), Robotics and Mechatronics Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine learning (ML), Soft Computing, Process Plant Automation, Real Time Control Systems Design, Controller design for Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV), Biomedical signal processing etc
